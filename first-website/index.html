Crawford

In a stunning performance, Terence Crawford secured a decisive win against Canelo Álvarez. His speed, precision, and ring IQ proved too much for Canelo, allowing Crawford to dominate the fight and make history as one of the greatest pound-for-pound boxers of his generation. Crawfords journey to the super middleweight championships involved

  • Ricky Burns — WBO Lightweight title
  • Thomas Dulorme — WBO Light Welterweight title
  • Julius Indongo — Undisputed Light Welterweight championship (all major belts)
  • Errol Spence Jr. — Undisputed Welterweight championship (all major belts)
  • Israil Madrimov — Light Middleweight titles
  • Canelo Álvarez — Undisputed Super Middleweight championship (WBA, WBC, IBF, WBO)
